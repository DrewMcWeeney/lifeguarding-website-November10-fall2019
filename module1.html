The Overview


In this module, you will learn the theoretical foundations of lifeguarding.


  • What is a lifeguard?
  • What is the role of a lifeguard?
  • What are lifeguards responsible for?
  • How do lifeguards function in aquatic systems?
  • What makes a professional lifeguard?
  • What makes an unprofessional lifeguard?
  • What equipment do lifeguards use?
  • What is water safety?


Through reading, writing, and participating as a class, we will explore these questions.
